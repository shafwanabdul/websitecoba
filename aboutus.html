    Yufa Technology Co., Ltd.

    Solution can solve your problem

    Founded in 2005, Yufa Technology Co., Ltd. began by developing and providing PU chemical machines and shoemaking machines, quickly establishing itself as a trusted partner in these industries. With a commitment to quality and innovation, Yufa Tech helped companies streamline production processes and improve manufacturing efficiency.

    As client needs expanded, Yufa Tech evolved into a comprehensive industrial solutions provider. Today, we specialize in offering integrated solutions tailored to a wide range of industries, ensuring that businesses receive well-planned, effective systems that enhance productivity and support sustainable growth.

    Corporate Social Responsibility

    Foreword: In 2021, the world ushered in the era of sustainable management, ESG ( Environment, Social, Corporate Governance) have become the focus of attention, It has also become an evaluation criterion for investors to evaluate the company's operations.

    With the vision of "the best production partner for shoemakers", Yufa gradually introduces ESG indicators and integrates them into the operation process on the basis of the existing CSR (Corporate Social Responsibility) business, internalizes the spirit of sustainability, effectively controls potential risks, improves operational efficiency, creates value for customers, and at the same time, through the combination of sustainability and core functions, in addition to providing customers with equipment and services to enhance production efficiency, It is also expected to bring stable and positive forces to the society and achieve the common good of the whole society.

    At present,Yufa gradually promotes ESG activities according to ESG indicators and their spirit, and forms standard operations and knowledge in existing operational activities.

    Finally, Yufa hopes to optimize its own operation and management constitution through the introduction of ESG, and hopes to enhance the competitiveness of customers through its own ability to provide quality services in line with ESG standards, and jointly bring more positive forces to society.
